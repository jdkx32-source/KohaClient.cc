Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 13, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how KohaClient ("we", "us", "our") handles information collected from users.

Home

1. Summary

KohaClient primarily integrates with Discord for Rich Presence. We do not collect personal data solely for Rich Presence functionality. This page explains any circumstances where data may be collected and how it is used.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • KohaClient does not collect or store Discord credentials or full payment card details on our servers for Rich Presence.
  • We do not collect game account passwords.

3. Information That May Be Collected

Depending on your use of optional features (for example, KC+ subscription), some personal data may be processed by third-party providers:

  • Account & Billing: If you subscribe to KC+ or purchase items, payment processors or store platforms may collect billing information. We do not store raw payment credentials on our servers.
  • Support & Contact: If you contact support (email or Discord), the messages you send will be stored to assist you.

4. Use of Information

  • To provide and maintain the Service (including Rich Presence and launcher updates).
  • To process optional subscription payments (handled via third parties).
  • To respond to support requests.

5. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services which may collect or process data under their own policies, for example:

  • Discord (API for Rich Presence)
  • Google Fonts and Boxicons (for fonts and icons)
  • Payment processors (if you subscribe to KC+)

We recommend reviewing those services' privacy policies directly for details.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

7. Your Rights

You may contact us to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you;
  • Request correction or deletion of your data;
  • Object to processing or request restrictions where applicable.

8. Children

The Service is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not intentionally collect personal information from children under the applicable age.

9. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data, but no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted here with a revised "Last updated" date.

11. Contact

If you have privacy questions or requests, contact: support@kohaclient.cc or on Discord: https://discord.kohaclient.cc/.
