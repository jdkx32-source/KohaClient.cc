Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about KohaClient.

What is KohaClient?

KohaClient is a modified Minecraft client designed to enhance performance, add new features, and personalize your overall gaming experience.

Is KohaClient free?

Yes, the base version of KohaClient is completely free. We also offer an optional premium subscription (KC+) that unlocks additional exclusive cosmetics and features.

How do I use KohaClient?

Simply download the launcher from our website, install it, and log in with your Minecraft account. The launcher will handle the rest, getting you into the game quickly.

Can I use my existing Minecraft mods?

Our launcher is built for compatibility, which is why we use Fabric as its foundation. While many popular mods work seamlessly, some may require specific configurations or might not be fully supported. We recommend checking our Discord for compatibility lists.

What if the launcher doesn't start?

Ensure you have the latest version of Java installed and your graphics drivers are up to date. If the issue persists, please contact our support team on Discord for assistance.

Which versions does KohaClient support?

At the moment, KohaClient supports all Minecraft Fabric versions from 1.21.8 to 1.21.9. We are continuously working to expand compatibility and make the client accessible to all types of users.
