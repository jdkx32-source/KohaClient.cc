FAQ

The essentials — short and clear.

What is KohaClient?
A modified Minecraft client for better performance, new features and maximum style.
Is KohaClient free?
Yes. The base is free. KC+ is optional and brings exclusive extras.
How do I use KohaClient?
Download the launcher, install, sign in with your MC account — done.
Mod compatibility?
We use Fabric. Many mods work out of the box. Details & lists are on Discord.
Launcher won't start?
Update Java & GPU drivers. If issues persist, get support on Discord.
Which versions are supported?
Currently Fabric 1.21.8 – 1.21.9. We are continuously expanding.
